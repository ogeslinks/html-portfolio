  7. NEWLY ADMITTED STUDENTS REQUIREMENTS FOR ONLINE CLEARANCE   -   Upon Successful Creation and Payment of the relevant acceptance Fee as listed below

    Documents required to be uploaded
    1. Birth Certificate or Age declaration
    2. WAEC or NECO/NABTEB result:- Statement of result or certificate
    (Certificates are expected from 2019 and below for WAEC, and 2017 and below for NECO, NABTEB)
    3. Scratch Card(s) to enable access to the above uploaded results NECO; WAEC; NABTEB: etc should be uploaded to enable confirmation of same.
    4. Local Government of origin Certificate or Affidavit of Local Government Area of origin
    5. Jamb result slip with passport
    6. Jamb admission letter (Institution copy)
    7. Acceptance letter
    8. Guarantor' s letter
    9. Affidavit of Good Conduct
    10. Affidavit of Non-Membership of secret cult
    11. A RED BACKGROUND PASSPORT AT THE SIDE OF THE JAMB BIOMETRIC PICTURE

  14. RETURNING STUDENTS SHOULD ENSURE THAT THEY HAVE MADE PAYMENT FOR THEIR CHARGES FOR PREVIOUS ACADEMIC SESSION!
    THEY ARE TO PAY THEIR RESPECTIVE CHARGES AND REGISTER THEIR COURSES FOR THE THE ACADEMIC SESSION.
    THEY ARE ALSO EXPECTED TO UPDATE THEIR PERSONAL DATA FORMS WITH INFORMATION SUCH AS PERMANENT ADDRESS;NEXT OF KIN; ETC.
    NOTE THAT THEY ARE TO THEN VISIT THEIR COURSE ADVISERS FOR VALIDATION OF REGISTERED COURSES.
    ONLY THEN CAN THEY PROCEED INTO THE NEW ACADEMIC SESSION

  15. RETURNING STUDENTS ARE EXPECTED TO MAKE PAYMENT OF CHARGES FOR THE ACTIVE ACADEMIC SESSION
    THEY ARE TO PAY THEIR RESPECTIVE CHARGES AND REGISTER THEIR COURSES FOR THE NEW ACADEMIC SESSION.
    NOTE THAT THEY ARE TO THEN VISIT THEIR COURSE ADVISERS FOR VALIDATION OF REGISTERED COURSES.
    CHARGE FOR LATE PAYMENT / REGISTRATION IS ACTIVATED FOR THE ACTIVE SESSION 5 WEEKS AFTER RESUMPTION.
    THE LATE CHARGE IS N10,0000
    TO AVOID THE ABOVE PENALTY, STUDENTS ARE THEREFORE ENCOURAGED TO PAY ON TIME

  16. NOTE THAT ALL STUDENTS ARE EXPECTED TO COMPLETE ALL THE INFORMATION REQUESTED IN THEIR PERSONAL DATA FORMS. FRESHERS SHOULD PARTICULARLY TAKE NOTE THAT FAILURE TO DO THIS MEANS THEY CANNOT RECEIVE THEIR UNIVERSITY IDENTITY CARDS
